Boiling an egg

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2 person (on diet)
Ingredients Quantity
Water, Eggs 250ml, 5

Step 1: Wash the eggs

Step 2: Put the eggs in a pot

Step 3: Fill the pot with water

Step 4: Turn on the stove, and put the pot onto it and let it boil for 10 mins

Step 5: Turn off the stove, take out the eggs and it,s ready to serve but dont forget to beel the shell off and then eat

Frying egg

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs, Oil, Pan 3, 15ml

Step 1: Wash the eggs

Step 2: Turn on the stove and put the pan onto it

Step 3: Put the oil in the pan and let it heat for abut a min

Step 4: Crack the eggs and put it in the pan and let the fry for 1:30 min
